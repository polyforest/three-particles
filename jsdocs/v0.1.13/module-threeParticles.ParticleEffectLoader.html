emissionScaling

Max particle counts and emission rates can be scaled by setting a scaling factor per emitter here. The key is the emitter id, the value is a numeric multiplier. To scale all emitters, use the key '*'. This can be used, for example, to reduce particle emissions on slower devices, or increase emissions for stress tests.
