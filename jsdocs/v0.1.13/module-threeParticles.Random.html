Class: Random

threeParticles.Random(seedopt)

A Pseudo-random number generator. Only meant to satisfy the requirements of particles without external dependencies. Borrowed from Blixt https://gist.github.com/blixt/f17b47c62508be59987b Which is in turn borrowed from Park-Miller PRNG.
